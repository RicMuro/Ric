Stockfish

  • Stockfish is currently the strongest open-source chess engine available to the public.
  • Stockfish 15, the current version, is rated 3585 elo. Around 700 points higher than the highest rated human, Magnus Carlsen.
  • It analyzes millions of moves per second combined together with an efficently updatable neural network (NNUE).
  • Stockfish has won the TCEC (Top Chess Engine Championship) multiple times in a row, placing 1st place since 2020.
